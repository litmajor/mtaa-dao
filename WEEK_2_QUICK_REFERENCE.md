## 🚀 How to Use

1. **Select a pool** from Pools tab
2. **Click** 💰 Performance tab
3. **View** APY, APR, fees, IL risk metrics
4. **Analyze** APY trend chart
5. **Study** fee tier distribution
6. **Adjust** hypothetical amount in calculator
7. **See** updated projections instantly
