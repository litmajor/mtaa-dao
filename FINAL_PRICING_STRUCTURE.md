## üéØ PRICING STRATEGY BY PHASE

### **Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-6)**

**Goal:** Prove product-market fit

**Promotional Pricing:**
- First 100 Growth customers: 50% off for 6 months (KES 150/month)
- First 20 Professional customers: 50% off for 6 months (KES 600/month)
- Founder pricing: Lock current rates for 24 months

**Success Metrics:**
- 500 active DAOs (any tier)
- 20 paid customers (4% conversion)
- KES 100k monthly recurring revenue
- <10% churn rate
