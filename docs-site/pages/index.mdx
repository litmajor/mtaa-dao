# Welcome to MtaaDAO 🌍

**MtaaDAO** is Africa's first AI-powered decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) platform designed for African communities. We're combining **blockchain**, **artificial intelligence**, and **mobile money** to democratize finance and governance.

## Govern Together, Grow Together

Every community deserves the power to **govern itself**, **pool resources**, and **make decisions together**—without intermediaries, without corruption, without barriers. 

Whether you're:
- **Organizing a Chama** (savings group)
- **Coordinating a Harambee** (community fundraiser)
- **Building a Regional Network** (MetaDAO federation)
- **Creating a Creative Collective** (artists, makers, builders)
- **Managing a Community Fund** (healthcare, education, development)

MtaaDAO gives you transparent, democratic, and auditable tools to do it.

**From Mtaa, For Mtaa.** Built by the people, for the people. 🌍

## 🚀 Get Started in 5 Minutes

### Step 1: Create Your DAO
Head to [Quick Start](/quick-start) and choose your DAO type:
- **Short-Term DAO** (30 days) - For events, funerals, or specific campaigns
- **Collective DAO** (Monthly) - For savings groups, business chamas, or community projects
- **MetaDAO** (Continuous) - For regional networks and strategic alliances

### Step 2: Set Up Governance
Define your rules:
- Who votes? (All members or just core team?)
- What's the vote threshold? (50%+1 or consensus?)
- How long do votes last? (1 day, 1 week?)

### Step 3: Activate Treasury
Connect your mobile money account or crypto wallet. Start receiving funds.

### Step 4: Invite Members
Share your unique invite link. They join with one click.

### Step 5: Make Decisions
Create proposals, vote, and execute automatically.

[**Launch Your DAO** →](/quick-start/create-dao)
