## Transaction Fees Explained

### Platform Fee (Pay-as-you-go)

We charge a small percentage of each transaction to cover:

- Security audits

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What if I can't afford ‚Ç≠1,500/month?

**Options:**
1. **Start with Free Tier** (‚Ç≠0) - Test with small group first
2. **Use Short-Term** (‚Ç≠500 one-time) - For temporary needs
3. **Share costs** - ‚Ç≠1,500 √∑ 50 members = ‚Ç≠30/member
4. **Start small, grow later** - Begin with Free, upgrade when treasury grows

### Can I pause my subscription?

**Short-Term:** No refunds, but can transfer to another DAO
**Collective:** 
- Can cancel anytime (stops at end of current month)
- Can pause for up to 3 months (‚Ç≠500 pause fee)
- Reactivation: Pay current month to resume

### Do you offer discounts?

**Yes! Discounts for:**
- Annual payment: 2 months free (17% off)
- Educational institutions: 25% off
- Non-profits: 30% off
- MetaDAOs (bulk): Custom pricing
- Early adopters: Grandfathered rates

**Apply for discount:** support@mtaadao.com

### What happens if I don't pay?

**Day 1-7:** Reminder emails/SMS  
**Day 8:** DAO enters "grace mode" (read-only, no new transactions)  
**Day 15:** DAO suspended (no access)  
**Day 30:** DAO archived (can reactivate within 90 days)  
**Day 120:** Permanent deletion (treasury returned to members)

**To reactivate:** Pay all outstanding fees + ‚Ç≠500 reactivation fee

### Are there hidden fees?

**NO hidden fees. Ever.**

You pay:
1. DAO tier subscription (‚Ç≠0-‚Ç≠1,500/month)
2. Transaction fees (2.5-3%)
3. Your payment method's standard fees (M-Pesa, bank, crypto gas)

That's it. We hate hidden fees as much as you do.
