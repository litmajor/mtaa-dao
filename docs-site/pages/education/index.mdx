## Key Concepts

**Blockchain**: A decentralized ledger where transactions are recorded in "blocks" and linked cryptographically.

**DeFi**: Financial services built on blockchain without banks—lending, borrowing, trading, and more.

**Smart Contract**: Self-executing code on blockchain that runs exactly as programmed.

**Token**: Digital asset on blockchain representing value, ownership, or utility.

**Wallet**: Software or hardware that stores your private keys and lets you interact with blockchain.
