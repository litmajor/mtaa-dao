## Extension Model: Strategic Hybrid

### Short-Term DAO Extensions

MtaaDAO uses a **Strategic Hybrid** approach:

1. **First Extension**: ₭500 → +15 days (half duration)
2. **Second Extension**: ₭500 → +7 days (half again)
3. **After 2 Extensions**: Auto-upgrade to Collective or close

**Why This Works**:
- ✅ Maintains urgency
- ✅ Fair revenue for platform
- ✅ Natural evolution to long-term plans
- ✅ Prevents ghost DAOs

### Example Timeline

```
Day 0:  Create DAO (₭500, 30 days)
Day 25: 1st Extension option (₭500, +15 days → expires Day 45)
Day 40: 2nd Extension option (₭500, +7 days → expires Day 52)
Day 52: Must upgrade to Collective (₭1,500/mo) or close
```

## Decision Framework

### Choose Short-Term DAO If:
- ✅ Clear end date (wedding, funeral, event)
- ✅ Specific fundraising goal
- ✅ Budget-conscious (₭500 vs ₭1,500)
- ✅ Temporary team
- ✅ One-time project

### Choose Collective DAO If:
- ✅ Ongoing operations (3+ months)
- ✅ Regular meetings/contributions
- ✅ Need full governance features
- ✅ Growing membership
- ✅ Multiple projects/vaults

### Upgrade to MetaDAO (Phase 2) If:
- ✅ Coordinating multiple DAOs
- ✅ Regional representation
- ✅ Strategic alliances
- ✅ Cross-DAO proposals

## Cost Analysis

### 3-Month Comparison

**Short-Term DAO Path**:
- Month 1: ₭500 (30 days)
- Extension 1: ₭500 (+15 days = Day 45)
- Extension 2: ₭500 (+7 days = Day 52)
- **Total**: ₭1,500 for ~52 days
- **Must upgrade after this**

**Collective DAO Path**:
- Month 1: ₭1,500
- Month 2: ₭1,500
- Month 3: ₭1,500
- **Total**: ₭4,500 for 90 days
- **Can continue indefinitely**

**Break-even**: ~52 days
