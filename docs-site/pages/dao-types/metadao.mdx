# MetaDAOs: Networks of DAOs

## Umoja ni Nguvu (Unity is Strength)

A **MetaDAO** is a network of multiple DAOs working together for collective power. Think of it as a "DAO of DAOs"‚Äîa federation where independent communities coordinate, pool resources, and amplify their impact.

---

## What is a MetaDAO?

Imagine 20 farmer cooperatives across Kenya. Individually, each has limited bargaining power. But together as a MetaDAO, they can:
- Negotiate bulk seed prices
- Share expensive equipment
- Access larger financing
- Export collectively
- Lobby for policy changes

**MetaDAOs** enable this coordination while preserving each DAO's independence.

## How MetaDAOs Work

### Joining a MetaDAO

**Requirements:**
1. Your DAO must be **Collective tier** or higher
2. At least 50 active members
3. 6+ months operational history
4. Good financial health (no defaults)
5. Approval by MetaDAO members (70% vote)

**Process:**
```
1. Apply to join MetaDAO
2. Pay joining fee (‚Ç≠5,000-‚Ç≠50,000 depending on MetaDAO)
3. Current member DAOs vote on your application
4. If approved, contribute to shared treasury
5. Gain voting power based on member count
```

### Voting Power

MetaDAOs use **quadratic voting** to balance small and large DAOs:

```
Voting Power = ‚àö(DAO Member Count)

Examples:
- 100-member DAO = 10 votes (‚àö100)
- 400-member DAO = 20 votes (‚àö400)
- 2,500-member DAO = 50 votes (‚àö2,500)
```

This prevents large DAOs from dominating while rewarding scale.

### Contribution Model

Member DAOs contribute to the MetaDAO treasury:

**Option 1: Percentage of Transactions**
```
Every transaction in your DAO contributes:
- 0.5% to MetaDAO treasury
- Example: ‚Ç≠100,000 transaction ‚Üí ‚Ç≠500 to MetaDAO
```

**Option 2: Fixed Monthly Dues**
```
Based on your DAO size:
- Small (50-200 members): ‚Ç≠2,500/month
- Medium (201-500): ‚Ç≠5,000/month
- Large (500+): ‚Ç≠10,000/month
```

**Option 3: Hybrid**
```
‚Ç≠1,000/month base + 0.2% of transactions
```

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can a DAO join multiple MetaDAOs?

Yes! DAOs can be members of multiple MetaDAOs. Example:
- Kisumu Farmers DAO ‚Üí Lake Region AgriMetaDAO
- Same DAO ‚Üí National Food Security MetaDAO
- Same DAO ‚Üí Climate Action MetaDAO

Each membership has separate dues and voting rights.

### What if DAOs disagree on MetaDAO proposals?

Member DAOs vote independently. A proposal passes at the MetaDAO level based on collective voting power, even if individual DAOs voted against it.

However, **opt-out clauses** allow DAOs to exclude themselves from specific proposals.

### Can a DAO leave a MetaDAO?

Yes. Process:
1. Submit exit proposal to MetaDAO
2. 30-day notice period
3. Settle any outstanding obligations
4. Receive pro-rata share of contributed capital
5. Lose access to MetaDAO resources

### How are disputes resolved?

MetaDAOs have built-in arbitration:
1. Internal mediation (Coordinating Council)
2. Member vote on dispute
3. External arbitration (if needed)
4. Smart contract-enforced resolution
