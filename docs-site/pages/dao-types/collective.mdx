
# Collective DAOs

Collective DAOs are designed for ongoing community initiatives, savings groups (chamas), businesses, and long-term projects.

## Overview

- **Monthly Fee**: ₭1,500/month (auto-renewable)
- **Duration**: Ongoing subscription
- **Best For**: Chamas, businesses, community projects

## Features

### Full DAO Toolkit

✅ **Governance**: Proposals, voting, multi-sig decisions  
✅ **Financial Management**: Multiple vaults, transparent treasury  
✅ **Member Management**: Unlimited members, role-based permissions  
✅ **Reporting**: Monthly performance summaries  
✅ **Integration**: Regional filters for MetaDAO linking  
✅ **Collaboration**: Merge with other DAOs

### Monthly Performance Summary

Every month, your Collective DAO receives:

- Total income & expenses
- Proposal success rate
- Member participation metrics
- Treasury health report
- Recommendations for growth

## Use Cases

### 1. Savings Chama
**Members**: 15-50 people  
**Goal**: Rotating savings & loans  
**Frequency**: Monthly contributions  
**Features Used**: Proposals for loans, voting on disbursements

### 2. Business Cooperative
**Members**: 5-20 entrepreneurs  
**Goal**: Pooled resources for bulk buying  
**Frequency**: Weekly meetings, monthly orders  
**Features Used**: Multi-sig treasury, proposal system

### 3. Community Development
**Members**: 100+ residents  
**Goal**: Neighborhood improvements  
**Frequency**: Quarterly projects  
**Features Used**: Transparent fund tracking, voting on priorities

## Upgrade from Short-Term DAO

When upgrading from a Short-Term DAO:

✅ **History Preserved**: All transactions, proposals, members  
✅ **Seamless Transition**: No data loss  
✅ **Feature Unlock**: Immediate access to full toolkit  
✅ **Prorated Billing**: First month adjusts for remaining short-term time

## When to Choose Collective DAO

✅ **Long-Term Vision**: Project lasts 3+ months  
✅ **Regular Activity**: Weekly/monthly operations  
✅ **Growth Plans**: Expect to add many members  
✅ **Complex Governance**: Need proposals, voting, reporting  
✅ **Multiple Projects**: Managing several initiatives

## Subscription Management

- **Auto-Renewal**: Charges from DAO treasury on renewal date
- **Cancellation**: Anytime with 7-day notice
- **Pause**: Temporarily freeze (max 3 months)
- **Downgrade**: Not available (archive and create new Short-Term)
