
# About MtaaDAO

## From Mtaa, For Mtaa

MtaaDAO is Afrika's first AI-powered decentralized autonomous organization (DAO) platform designed specifically for African communities. We're combining **blockchain**, **artificial intelligence**, and **mobile money** to democratize finance and governance.

---

## Our Story

MtaaDAO was born from lived experience — from failed football fundraisers, chaotic funeral collections, and the frustration of watching communities rich in trust but poor in structure.
